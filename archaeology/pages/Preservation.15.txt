==Migration==
Over long periods of time, preserving data means migrating it. Whether it is locked in a social network, or stored in an old file format, migration must be done.

Migrating from cloud services is a big area of concern right now. Several [[Tools]] have been created to do this, and Google has opened its [[Data Liberation Front|http://www.dataliberation.org/]].
