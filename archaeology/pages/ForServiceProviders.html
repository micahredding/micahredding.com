This is a collection of basic principles that all social networks, cloud services, and software creators should honor.

  • Customer lock-in through holding data hostage is not an acceptable business model.
  • Don't delete user data without permission.
  • Don't destroy metadata.
  • Offer clear options for export or migration.
  • These exports should contain all content and metadata.
  • If you are closing shop, give at least 90 days notice, and provide clear ways to export all user-generated content.

These should be the standards by which we determine whether we will use your service at all.
