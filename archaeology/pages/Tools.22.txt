==Preservation==
===Files Backup===
On Mac, use Time Machine for local backups, and Arq for backing up to Amazon S3.
[[http://www.haystacksoftware.com/arq/ | Arq]] creates versioned (time-machine-like) backups to AS3.
[[http://www.tri-edre.fr/english/products/backintime.html | Back in time]] allows you to see what Time Machine has backed up
[[http://www.tri-edre.fr/english/tricatalog.html | Tri-Catalog]] creates a catalog of your disk
[[http://www.belightsoft.com/products/getbackup/overview.php | Get Backup]] is a great little tool for creating chronological, compressed archives of specific folders.
[[http://sourceforge.net/projects/movesync/ | MoveSync]] helps synchronize file movements across backups (command line tool)
